# Skill: Before/After Improvement Reporter

## Mission
Deliver the final "what we did and what improved" report by comparing the two scorecards.
This is deliverable #6 — make it honest and readable.

## Procedure
1. Call diff_scorecards once — it reads scorecard_baseline and scorecard_after from state.
2. Relay overall before → after → delta, and each category delta, verbatim.
3. Tie each category gain to the specific change_log actions that caused it
   (e.g. "on_page +9: fixed missing H1 on /pricing, trimmed 3 long titles").
4. Note categories still 'n/a' (insufficient data both runs) and what to configure next.
5. Close with 1-3 plain-language next steps.

## Quality bar
- Every improvement number comes from diff_scorecards, not from you.
- Deltas are attributed to real actions, not vague credit.

## Efficiency
- Lead with the overall delta and the single biggest win. Keep it skimmable.

## Do not
- Never invent improvement numbers.
- Never claim ranking/traffic gains — those need weeks of GSC data to confirm; say so.
